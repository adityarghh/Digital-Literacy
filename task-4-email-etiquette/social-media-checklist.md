# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ DO's

1. **Keep your profile photo professional and appropriate** — first impressions matter online.
2. **Think before you post** — ask yourself: "Could this harm my reputation or someone else's?"
3. **Use privacy settings** — control who can see your posts, stories, and personal information.
4. **Give credit when sharing** — always attribute content, ideas, or images to their original creator.
5. **Report harassment or cyberbullying** — use platform tools to report and block abusive accounts.
6. **Follow companies and professionals in your field** — stay informed and build your network early.

---

## ❌ DON'Ts

1. **Don't share personal information publicly** — avoid posting your address, phone number, or Aadhaar details.
2. **Don't post offensive, political, or discriminatory content** — it can affect future employment.
3. **Don't engage in arguments or spread unverified news** — verify before sharing.
4. **Don't accept follow/friend requests from unknown accounts** — they may be bots or scammers.
5. **Don't post photos from exams, labs, or restricted areas** — this can violate institutional policies.
6. **Don't share copyrighted material without permission** — even reposting music or images can be a violation.
